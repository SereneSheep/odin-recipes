Stir-Fried Rice

Description

This is a recipe for stir-fried rice. Stir-fried rice involves first cooking whichever ingredients you want to complement the rice with, like protein, legumes, etc., and then stir-frying (preferably leftover) rice without together with the other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Rice

    • This is probably leftover rice, though recently-cooked rice can be used in a pinch. Remember, rice does not keep for a long time in the fridge.

  • Protein

    • This could be, for example, chicken, shrimp, salmon or tofu.

  • Veggies

    • Peas, broccoli, bellpepper, and so on.

  • Cooking oil

    • Sesame oil is the traditional and preferred choice, but olive oil or vegetable oil can do in a pinch

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet or a wok with the cooking oil.
  2. Add the protein.
  3. Add the veggies.
  4. Add a bit more oil and stir in the rice.
  5. Keep cooking and stiring for a few minutes, until the rice is fried.
