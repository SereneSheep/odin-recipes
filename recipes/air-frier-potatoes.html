Air-Fried Potatoes

Description

Simple recipe for air-frier baked potatoes - bake potatoes in a fraction of the time!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes

    • Cooking time varies depending on potato size. Aim for around 20 minutes for baby potatoes.

  • Garlic powder
  • Salt
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, coat the potatoes in around 1 tablespoon of olive oil.
  2. Sprinkle salt on the potatoes.
  3. Pre-heat the air fryer at 200ÂºC for 5 minutes.
  4. Cook the potatoes in the air-fryer for 20 minutes, flipping half-way through.
  5. Remove the potatoes from the air-frier and coat them with the garlic powder in a bowl.
  6. Let rest for 5-10 minutes and serve.
